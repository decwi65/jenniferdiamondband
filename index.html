The Band

The Band

The Jennifer Diamond Band is a dynamic five-piece unit known for their powerhouse vocals and infectious energy which lights up the stage. Led by the mighty Jennifer Diamond on vocals, the band also features two other powerhouse vocalists, guitarists Richard Crooks and Bill Poirier. They all come together in amazing harmony! Rounding out the line-up is our grooving bass player Keith Henderson and Curtis Michael Leippi, a drummer with both power and finesse.

Let the Jennifer Diamond Band light up your pub, wedding or party. We will have heads bobbing and feet moving from the very first song.
