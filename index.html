The Band

The Band

The Jennifer Diamond Band is a dynamic five-piece known for their powerhouse vocals and infectious energy to light up every stage they perform on. Led by their powerhouse female lead vocalist, Jennifer Diamond, the band also features two other vocalists, guitarists Richard Crooks and Bill Poirier, resulting in great harmonies and versatility. Rounding out the line up are the talented bass player Keith Henderson and powerful drummer Curtis Michael Leippi.

Let the Jennifer Diamond Band light up your pub, wedding or party. We will have heads bobbing and feet moving from the very first song.
